Theodore Farland

I'm Theodore Farland – known in the indie game dev world as LuggageGoblin – a multidisciplinary software developer, game designer, and artist. I blend creative tinkering with professional rigor to reimagine classic game concepts, craft interactive experiences, and produce 3D models that I print and paint by hand. With a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science (and a minor in Mathematics) from ECSU, I thrive on solving tough problems with an eye for detail.
