Escape The Light

About the Game

Escape the Light is a stealth / puzzle game where you play as a shadow that uses alchemy abilities to avoid being extinguished by the Light Prisms.

Controls

  • Move: WASD / Left Analog
  • Confirm: Space / A or X
  • Abilities: 1,2,3,4 or Arrow Keys / D-pad
  • Rotate Wall: Q & E / R1 & L1 or Left and Right Bumpers
