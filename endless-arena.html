Endless Arena

About the Game

Endless Arena is a simple little survival game where the player has to survive hoards of enemies.

Controls

  • Move: WASD / Left Analog
  • Dash: Space / A or X
  • Attack:Arrow Keys / Right Analog
  • Swap Attack: Q & E / R1 & L1 or Left and Right Bumpers
  • Pause:P & Escape / Start
  • Shop:Mouse
